Pasta Primavera Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add bell pepper, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  5. Mix in the cooked pasta and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Top with grated parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!
